Who Is It?

Lisa is looking for a new lease on life. She realizes that she never really does what she wants to do. She wanted to be in theater, but her parents wanted her to go to college and get a "real job". She has a never give up personality and you can expect her to help you if you need it.

  • 26 years old
  • Miami, Florida
  • Head of Space Pixie Human Resources

What Goals?

  • Start living for herself.
  • Use Space Pixie to take a trip into space.
  • Possibly never come back, once she's in space.

What Attitude?

Lisa thinks the company itself has it perks, but she's seen what it's really like on the inside. She expects the service to be top notch, after she handles all the politics involved with going into outer space. Lisa has seen how happy and how people have changed after their trip into space and she wants that to happen to her.

Which Behaviour?

  • She believes in getting what she pays for.
  • Lisa paid for a trip to the Bahamas through an startup called WeShipIt. They promised all sorts of things in the pre-packaged service. She carefully read over all the documents she signed before leaving for her trip. After she got back, she sued them into bankruptcy because they didn't deliver one of the services in the package.
  • Lisa only takes in information from reliable and trusted sources, like certain newspapers and websites.
  • If a service that is being promised is too good to be true, Lisa won't even try it.
  • If all the services add up to her reasonable ball park range, she will try it.
